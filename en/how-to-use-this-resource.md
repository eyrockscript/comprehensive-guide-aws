# How to Use This Resource

This guide is structured to serve as both a progressive learning resource and a practical reference. Here's how to get the most out of it:

- **Sequential Reading**: If you're new to AWS or seeking a comprehensive understanding, we recommend starting with Section I (AWS Fundamentals) and progressing chapter by chapter. Each section builds upon the previous one, from basic concepts to advanced solutions.
- **Targeted Consultation**: If you already have experience with AWS, you can go directly to the chapters that address your specific needs, such as security (Chapter 5), integration (Chapter 6), or cost strategies (Chapter 10). The index will facilitate navigation.
- **Practical Application**: Each chapter includes executive summaries, best practices, and additional references so you can apply what you've learned to real-world projects.
- **Bilingual Exploration**: This content is available in Spanish (`es/` folder) and English (`en/` folder), allowing you to choose your preferred language or compare technical terms between both.

We recommend complementing your reading with the official AWS documentation and experimenting directly in the console to reinforce your learning.
